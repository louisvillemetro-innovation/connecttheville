 Is your company in the process of upgrading your computers? Do you have old computers lying around the office that no one is using? If so, consider donating them to those in need!

Since 2017, Louisville Metro and JCPS have partnered with companies local banks and corporations to collect used computers for redistribution to Louisville residents. Once we receive the donated computers, we send them to the students in the computer science program at Fern Creek High School, who refurbish them as part of their curriculum. The refurbished computers are then distributed to public housing residents and other citizens on the waiting list. Currently, there are over 400 people who are on the waiting list for a computer.

If your company would like to make a computer donation, please use the below form or contact Ed Blayney at: edward.blayney@louisvilleky.gov

At this time, we are only accepting computers from corporate donors. No individual donations can be accepted. However, you can still get involved! If you would like to be added to our digital inclusion mailing list, please click here, and please consider making a tax-deductible donation to our Digital Inclusion Fund.
